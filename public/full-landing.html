Reimagining the Odds: The Crypto Lottery DApp

Inspired by a personal connection with her late father — who always encouraged her to "try her luck" — Tatianne started thinking about how we define chance and opportunity in today's world.

She discovered the odds of winning a traditional lottery are about 1 in 14 million. Even blockchain-based versions like PancakeSwap Lottery improve that only to 1 in 1 million.

So she asked:

Enter the Crypto Lottery DApp

Built by Yuca Media with 1 in 1 million odds of winning. But wait, that's the same as PancakeSwap Lottery. How is your CryptoLottery_Dapp be different and better? That's easy: Lower fees.

PancakeSwap Lottery is built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), and the CryptoLottery_Dapp is built on the Solana Blockchain. While both offer relatively low transaction costs compared to Ethereum, Solana's fees are typically even lower, ranging from a few thousandths of a cent to a few cents per transaction.

Players can pick 5 numbers from 1-30: C(30,5) = that's 142,506 combinations. And 1 bonus number from 1-7: 7 combinations. The Total: 142,506 × 7 = 997,542 (odds of ~1 in 997,542), bringing the odds to approximately 1 in 1 million.

With jackpots powered by blockchain and transparent smart contracts, we are creating a lottery where people stand a chance.

Yuca Studios: Giving Creators Their Power Back

As a filmmaker and actress herself, Tatianne saw another problem:

Production is drying up. Artists are being priced out. And many creative professionals — including herself — are facing eviction or financial collapse.

Yuca Studios is our solution.

A blockchain-powered production studio focused on keeping films and jobs in California, while creating new pathways for independent artists and vulnerable creatives to thrive.

This isn't just about film. It's about rebuilding an industry with community ownership, financial transparency, and tech-driven opportunity.
